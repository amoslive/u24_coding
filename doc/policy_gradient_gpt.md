<h2 data-section-id="av40jk" data-start="57" data-end="87">先说一句：Policy Gradient 到底想干嘛？</h2>
<p data-start="89" data-end="101">它想做的事其实特别朴素：</p>
<p data-start="103" data-end="146"><strong data-start="103" data-end="146">“哪些动作最后结果好，我以后就更爱选它；哪些动作最后结果差，我以后就少选它。”</strong></p>
<p data-start="148" data-end="154">就这一句话。</p>
<hr data-start="156" data-end="159" />
<h2 data-section-id="r6qs2b" data-start="161" data-end="180">先别看公式，先看一个最傻瓜的例子</h2>
<p data-start="182" data-end="196">假设你在玩一个很简单的游戏：</p>
<p data-start="198" data-end="217">你每次看到一个路口，都只能选两个动作：</p>
<ul data-start="219" data-end="228">
<li data-section-id="yhvaea" data-start="219" data-end="223">
<p data-start="221" data-end="223">左转</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="yhvmjb" data-start="224" data-end="228">
<p data-start="226" data-end="228">右转</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="230" data-end="241">你的策略网络现在输出：</p>
<ul data-start="243" data-end="264">
<li data-section-id="1drdqcl" data-start="243" data-end="253">
<p data-start="245" data-end="253">左转概率 70%</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="k17idw" data-start="254" data-end="264">
<p data-start="256" data-end="264">右转概率 30%</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="266" data-end="282">也就是说，它现在“更偏爱左转”。</p>
<p data-start="284" data-end="331">然后你真的按这个概率采样了一次，结果这回你选了<strong data-start="307" data-end="313">右转</strong>，最后居然中了大奖，拿到了很高奖励。</p>
<p data-start="333" data-end="340">那你会怎么想？</p>
<p data-start="342" data-end="346">很自然：</p>
<p data-start="348" data-end="392"><strong data-start="348" data-end="392">“哦，原来这个情况下右转挺好，那下次这个情况出现时，我应该让右转更容易被选到。”</strong></p>
<p data-start="394" data-end="418">这就是 policy gradient 的核心。</p>
<p data-start="420" data-end="446">反过来，如果你这次选了右转，结果死得很惨，那就应该：</p>
<p data-start="448" data-end="461"><strong data-start="448" data-end="461">“下次少选右转。”</strong></p>
<hr data-start="463" data-end="466" />
<h2 data-section-id="1q4qppw" data-start="468" data-end="498">所以 policy gradient 本质上在调什么？</h2>
<p data-start="500" data-end="507">它直接调的是：</p>
<p data-start="509" data-end="521"><strong data-start="509" data-end="520">策略本身的参数</strong>。</p>
<p data-start="523" data-end="531">也就是这个东西：</p>
<span class="katex-display" style=""><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML" display="block"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi>π</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>a</mi><mi mathvariant="normal">∣</mi><mi>s</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\pi_\theta(a|s)</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 1em; vertical-align: -0.25em;"></span><span class="mord"><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.03588em;">π</span><span class="msupsub"><span class="vlist-t vlist-t2"><span class="vlist-r"><span class="vlist" style="height: 0.3361em;"><span style="top: -2.55em; margin-left: -0.0359em; margin-right: 0.05em;"><span class="pstrut" style="height: 2.7em;"></span><span class="sizing reset-size6 size3 mtight"><span class="mord mathnormal mtight" style="margin-right: 0.02778em;">θ</span></span></span></span><span class="vlist-s">​</span></span><span class="vlist-r"><span class="vlist" style="height: 0.15em;"><span></span></span></span></span></span></span><span class="mopen">(</span><span class="mord mathnormal">a</span><span class="mord">∣</span><span class="mord mathnormal">s</span><span class="mclose">)</span></span></span></span></span>
<p data-start="556" data-end="559">读作：</p>
<blockquote data-start="561" data-end="608">
<p data-start="563" data-end="608">在状态 <span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>s</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">s</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.4306em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal">s</span></span></span></span> 下，采取动作 <span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>a</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">a</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.4306em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal">a</span></span></span></span> 的概率，由参数 <span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>θ</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\theta</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.6944em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.02778em;">θ</span></span></span></span> 决定。</p>
</blockquote>
<p data-start="610" data-end="655">比如神经网络输入状态，输出每个动作的概率。<br data-start="631" data-end="634" />
这个网络的参数就是 <span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>θ</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\theta</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.6944em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.02778em;">θ</span></span></span></span>。</p>
<p data-start="657" data-end="681">所以 policy gradient 不是在说：</p>
<ul data-start="683" data-end="706">
<li data-section-id="1u05k0q" data-start="683" data-end="694">
<p data-start="685" data-end="694">“这个状态值多少”</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="k0wazd" data-start="695" data-end="706">
<p data-start="697" data-end="706">“这个动作值多少”</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="708" data-end="715">而是在直接说：</p>
<p data-start="717" data-end="746"><strong data-start="717" data-end="746">“我怎么改网络参数，才能让好动作更容易被采样到？”</strong></p>
<hr data-start="748" data-end="751" />
<h2 data-section-id="y1vnp" data-start="753" data-end="770">为什么叫 Gradient？</h2>
<p data-start="772" data-end="787">因为我们想用梯度上升去改参数：</p>
<span class="katex-display" style=""><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML" display="block"><semantics><mrow><mi>θ</mi><mo>←</mo><mi>θ</mi><mo>+</mo><mi>α</mi><msub><mi mathvariant="normal">∇</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><mi>J</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>θ</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\theta \leftarrow \theta + \alpha \nabla_\theta J(\theta)</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.6944em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.02778em;">θ</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.2778em;"></span><span class="mrel">←</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.2778em;"></span></span><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.7778em; vertical-align: -0.0833em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.02778em;">θ</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.2222em;"></span><span class="mbin">+</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.2222em;"></span></span><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 1em; <p data-start="854" data-end="858">意思是：</p>
<ul data-start="860" data-end="947">
<li data-section-id="1l3fref" data-start="860" data-end="886">
<p data-start="862" data-end="886"><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>J</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>θ</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">J(\theta)</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 1em; vertical-align: -0.25em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.09618em;">J</span><span class="mopen">(</span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.02778em;">θ</span><span class="mclose">)</span></span></span></span>：当前策略的表现有多好</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="4fujkj" data-start="887" data-end="931">
<p data-start="889" data-end="931"><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi mathvariant="normal">∇</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><mi>J</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>θ</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\nabla_\theta J(\theta)</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 1em; vertical-align: -0.25em;"></span><span class="mord"><span class="mord">∇</span><span class="msupsub"><span class="vlist-t vlist-t2"><span class="vlist-r"><span class="vlist" style="height: 0.3361em;"><span style="top: -2.55em; margin-left: 0em; margin-right: 0.05em;"><span class="pstrut" style="height: 2.7em;"></span><span class="sizing reset-size6 size3 mtight"><span class="mord mathnormal mtight" style="margin-right: 0.02778em;">θ</span></span></span></span><span class="vlist-s">​</span></span><span class="vlist-r"><span class="vlist" style="height: 0.15em;"><span></span></span></span></span></span></span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.09618em;">J</span><span class="mopen">(</span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.02778em;">θ</span><span class="mclose">)</span></span></span></span>：往哪个方向改参数，表现会变好</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="1qxy5vl" data-start="932" data-end="947">
<p data-start="934" data-end="947"><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>α</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\alpha</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.4306em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.0037em;">α</span></span></span></span>：步长</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="949" data-end="958">所以名字拆开就是：</p>
<ul data-start="960" data-end="993">
<li data-section-id="ulkym9" data-start="960" data-end="975">

</li>
<li data-section-id="14j2va5" data-start="976" data-end="993">

</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="995" data-end="999">合起来：</p>
<p data-start="1001" data-end="1014"><strong data-start="1001" data-end="1014">用梯度去优化策略。</strong></p>
<hr data-start="1016" data-end="1019" />
<h2 data-section-id="lb5bkz" data-start="1021" data-end="1043">那最关键的问题来了：梯度到底长什么样？</h2>
<p data-start="1045" data-end="1055">最经典的一条式子是：</p>

<p data-start="1154" data-end="1184">第一次看到这玩意儿，正常人都会懵。<br data-start="1171" data-end="1174" />
我们把它翻译成人话。</p>

<h2 data-section-id="k2d5ud" data-start="1191" data-end="1202">这条公式的人话版</h2>

<p data-start="1250" data-end="1263">这坨东西可以拆成两部分看：</p>
<h3 data-section-id="u7pfa6" data-start="1265" data-end="1274">第一部分：</h3>
<span class="katex-display" style=""><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML" display="block"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi mathvariant="normal">∇</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><mi>log</mi><mo>⁡</mo><msub><mi>π</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>a</mi><mi mathvariant="normal">∣</mi><mi>s</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\nabla_\theta \log \pi_\theta(a|s)</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 1em; <p data-start="1317" data-end="1326">它的意思近似就是：</p>
<p data-start="1328" data-end="1369"><strong data-start="1328" data-end="1369">“如果我要让这次选中的动作 <span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>a</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">a</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.4306em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal">a</span></span></span></span> 更可能再次出现，我该怎么改参数？”</strong></p>
<p data-start="1371" data-end="1392">注意，是“<strong data-start="1376" data-end="1390">这次已经选出来的动作</strong>”。</p>
<hr data-start="1394" data-end="1397" />
<h3 data-section-id="ssks5u" data-start="1399" data-end="1408">第二部分：</h3>
<span class="katex-display"><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML" display="block"><semantics><mrow><mi>R</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">R</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.6833em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.00773em;">R</span></span></span></span></span>
<p data-start="1418" data-end="1433">它表示这次动作最后带来的回报。</p>
<ul data-start="1435" data-end="1481">
<li data-section-id="sk3vxy" data-start="1435" data-end="1456">
<p data-start="1437" data-end="1456">如果 <span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>R</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">R</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.6833em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.00773em;">R</span></span></span></span> 很大，说明这次结果好</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="13sbriz" data-start="1457" data-end="1481">
<p data-start="1459" data-end="1481">如果 <span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>R</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">R</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.6833em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.00773em;">R</span></span></span></span> 很小甚至负，说明这次结果差</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr data-start="1483" data-end="1486" />
<h3 data-section-id="phuxqw" data-start="1488" data-end="1498">合起来就是：</h3>
<ul data-start="1500" data-end="1584">
<li data-section-id="1r5cjpu" data-start="1500" data-end="1541">
<p data-start="1502" data-end="1541">这次动作结果好：<br data-start="1510" data-end="1513" />
<strong data-start="1515" data-end="1541">就朝着‘让这个动作以后更容易出现’的方向更新</strong></p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="whuqfg" data-start="1542" data-end="1584">
<p data-start="1544" data-end="1584">这次动作结果差：<br data-start="1552" data-end="1555" />
<strong data-start="1557" data-end="1584">就朝着‘让这个动作以后更不容易出现’的方向更新</strong></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="1586" data-end="1601">所以这公式根本不神秘，它就是：</p>
<blockquote data-start="1603" data-end="1623">
<p data-start="1605" data-end="1623"><strong data-start="1605" data-end="1623">奖励好的动作，惩罚差的动作。</strong></p>
</blockquote>
<hr data-start="1625" data-end="1628" />
<h2 data-section-id="go029v" data-start="1630" data-end="1642">为什么是 log？</h2>
<p data-start="1644" data-end="1656">这个地方最容易把人劝退。</p>
<p data-start="1658" data-end="1673">你先不用管数学推导，先记结论：</p>
<p data-start="1675" data-end="1676">用</p>
<span class="katex-display" style=""><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML" display="block"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi mathvariant="normal">∇</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><mi>log</mi><mo>⁡</mo><msub><mi>π</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>a</mi><mi mathvariant="normal">∣</mi><mi>s</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\nabla_\theta \log \pi_\theta(a|s)</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 1em; <p data-start="1720" data-end="1724">比直接用</p>
<span class="katex-display" style=""><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML" display="block"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi mathvariant="normal">∇</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><msub><mi>π</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>a</mi><mi mathvariant="normal">∣</mi><mi>s</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\nabla_\theta \pi_\theta(a|s)</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 1em; <p data-start="1763" data-end="1772">更好算，也更稳定。</p>
<p data-start="1774" data-end="1782">它本质上是在问：</p>
<p data-start="1784" data-end="1810"><strong data-start="1784" data-end="1810">“怎样改参数，能相对地提高这次动作的概率？”</strong></p>
<p data-start="1812" data-end="1833">你可以把它理解成一个“调概率偏好”的工具。</p>
<hr data-start="1835" data-end="1838" />
<h2 data-section-id="1osstpr" data-start="1840" data-end="1852">一个更具体的小例子</h2>
<p data-start="1854" data-end="1861">假设某状态下：</p>
<ul data-start="1863" data-end="1884">
<li data-section-id="1drdyap" data-start="1863" data-end="1873">
<p data-start="1865" data-end="1873">左转概率 0.8</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="k17kpq" data-start="1874" data-end="1884">
<p data-start="1876" data-end="1884">右转概率 0.2</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="1886" data-end="1913">这次采样到了右转，最后拿到很高奖励 <span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>R</mi><mo>=</mo><mn>10</mn></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">R=10</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.6833em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.00773em;">R</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.2778em;"></span><span class="mrel">=</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.2778em;"></span></span><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.6444em;"></span><span class="mord">10</span></span></span></span>。</p>
<p data-start="1915" data-end="1925">那更新时就会倾向于：</p>
<ul data-start="1927" data-end="1952">
<li data-section-id="ke0ssf" data-start="1927" data-end="1938">
<p data-start="1929" data-end="1938">提高“右转”的概率</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="t7jhwg" data-start="1939" data-end="1952">
<p data-start="1941" data-end="1952">相对降低“左转”的概率</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="1954" data-end="1966">过几次以后，也许就变成：</p>
<ul data-start="1968" data-end="1985">
<li data-section-id="1vyx3fu" data-start="1968" data-end="1976">
<p data-start="1970" data-end="1976">左转 0.6</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="lduazh" data-start="1977" data-end="1985">
<p data-start="1979" data-end="1985">右转 0.4</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="1987" data-end="2009">如果右转继续老是拿高奖励，最后甚至可能变成：</p>
<ul data-start="2011" data-end="2028">
<li data-section-id="1vyx3fy" data-start="2011" data-end="2019">
<p data-start="2013" data-end="2019">左转 0.2</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="lduaz5" data-start="2020" data-end="2028">
<p data-start="2022" data-end="2028">右转 0.8</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="2030" data-end="2055">这就是 policy gradient 在干的事。</p>
<p data-start="2057" data-end="2078">它不是一上来就知道哪个动作好。<br data-start="2072" data-end="2075" />
它是：</p>
<p data-start="2080" data-end="2100"><strong data-start="2080" data-end="2100">先试，试完以后，根据结果改偏好。</strong></p>
<hr data-start="2102" data-end="2105" />
<h2 data-section-id="1ysnasj" data-start="2107" data-end="2133">它和“Q-learning”那种方法有啥区别？</h2>
<p data-start="2135" data-end="2142">非常粗暴地讲：</p>
<h3 data-section-id="65fau5" data-start="2144" data-end="2167">Value-based（比如 DQN）</h3>
<p data-start="2168" data-end="2176">先学一个评分员：</p>
<blockquote data-start="2178" data-end="2190">
<p data-start="2180" data-end="2190">“这个动作值几分？”</p>
</blockquote>
<p data-start="2192" data-end="2199">然后选分高的。</p>
<hr data-start="2201" data-end="2204" />
<h3 data-section-id="1kepyjv" data-start="2206" data-end="2242">Policy-based（比如 Policy Gradient）</h3>
<p data-start="2243" data-end="2254">不先拐弯评分，直接学：</p>
<blockquote data-start="2256" data-end="2273">
<p data-start="2258" data-end="2273">“我该以多大概率选这个动作？”</p>
</blockquote>
<p data-start="2275" data-end="2284">所以你可以理解为：</p>
<ul data-start="2286" data-end="2336">
<li data-section-id="1cu5k8s" data-start="2286" data-end="2302">
<p data-start="2288" data-end="2302">DQN 更像“先打分，再选”</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="klfeeo" data-start="2303" data-end="2336">
<p data-start="2305" data-end="2336">Policy Gradient 更像“直接训练出一个行动习惯”</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr data-start="2338" data-end="2341" />
<h2 data-section-id="1pszxsa" data-start="2343" data-end="2356">训练过程到底怎么跑？</h2>

<h2 data-section-id="11y4vd" data-start="2755" data-end="2769">为什么它有时候不稳定？</h2>
<p data-start="2771" data-end="2782">因为它有个很烦的问题：</p>
<p data-start="2784" data-end="2792"><strong data-start="2784" data-end="2792">方差大。</strong></p>
<p data-start="2794" data-end="2799">举个例子：</p>
<p data-start="2801" data-end="2871">你第 1 步选了一个动作，最后整局赢了。<br data-start="2821" data-end="2824" />
但你真的能说“赢就是因为第 1 步这个动作特别好吗”？<br data-start="2851" data-end="2854" />
不一定。可能是后面某一步救回来的。</p>
<p data-start="2873" data-end="2899">所以 policy gradient 会有一种问题：</p>
<p data-start="2901" data-end="2929"><strong data-start="2901" data-end="2929">它会把整局的好坏，比较粗暴地分摊到每一步动作上。</strong></p>
<p data-start="2931" data-end="2946">这就会导致训练抖、慢、不稳定。</p>

margin-left: -0.0359em; margin-right: 0.05em;"><span class="pstrut" style="height: 2.7em;"></span><span class="sizing reset-size6 size3 mtight"><span class="mord mathnormal mtight" style="margin-right: 0.02778em;">θ</span></span></span></span><span class="vlist-s">​</span></span><span class="vlist-r"><span class="vlist" style="height: 0.15em;"><span></span></span></span></span></span></span><span class="mopen">(</span><span class="mord mathnormal">a</span><span class="mord">∣</span><span class="mord mathnormal">s</span><span class="mclose">)</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.1667em;"></span><span class="mopen">(</span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.00773em;">R</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.2222em;"></span><span class="mbin">−</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.2222em;"></span></span><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 1em; <p data-start="3105" data-end="3126">这里 <span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>b</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">b</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.6944em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal">b</span></span></span></span> 就是 baseline。</p>
<p data-start="3128" data-end="3146">你可以把它理解成一个“正常水平线”。</p>
<hr data-start="3148" data-end="3151" />
<h3 data-section-id="ly2vzm" data-start="3153" data-end="3162">人话解释：</h3>
<p data-start="3164" data-end="3202">不是说你拿到奖励就一定表扬，<br data-start="3178" data-end="3181" />
而是看你这次奖励<strong data-start="3189" data-end="3201">有没有比平时更好</strong>。</p>
<ul data-start="3204" data-end="3265">
<li data-section-id="1216vxe" data-start="3204" data-end="3221">
<p data-start="3206" data-end="3221">比 baseline 好：鼓励</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="11jd2jv" data-start="3222" data-end="3239">
<p data-start="3224" data-end="3239">比 baseline 差：打压</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="oaau3j" data-start="3240" data-end="3265">
<p data-start="3242" data-end="3265">跟 baseline 差不多：别太激动，少更新</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="3267" data-end="3283">这就比“只看原始奖励”聪明很多。</p>
<hr data-start="3285" data-end="3288" />
<h2 data-section-id="1r81dz2" data-start="3290" data-end="3308">Advantage 又是什么？</h2>
<p data-start="3310" data-end="3320">再往后一步，就会有：</p>
<span class="katex-display"><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML" display="block"><semantics><mrow><mi>A</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>s</mi><mo separator="true">,</mo><mi>a</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo><mo>=</mo><mi>Q</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>s</mi><mo separator="true">,</mo><mi>a</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo><mo>−</mo><mi>V</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>s</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">A(s,a) = Q(s,a) - V(s)</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 1em; vertical-align: -0.25em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal">A</span><span class="mopen">(</span><span class="mord mathnormal">s</span><span class="mpunct">,</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.1667em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal">a</span><span class="mclose">)</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.2778em;"></span><span class="mrel">=</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.2778em;"></span></span><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 1em; vertical-align: -0.25em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal">Q</span><span class="mopen">(</span><span class="mord mathnormal">s</span><span class="mpunct">,</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.1667em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal">a</span><span class="mclose">)</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.2222em;"></span><span class="mbin">−</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.2222em;"></span></span><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 1em; vertical-align: -0.25em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.22222em;">V</span><span class="mopen">(</span><span class="mord mathnormal">s</span><span class="mclose">)</span></span></span></span></span>
<p data-start="3352" data-end="3358">它的意思是：</p>
<p data-start="3360" data-end="3387"><strong data-start="3360" data-end="3387">这个动作，比这个状态下的平均水平，到底好多少。</strong></p>
<p data-start="3389" data-end="3408">所以 advantage 的人话就是：</p>
<blockquote data-start="3410" data-end="3428">
<p data-start="3412" data-end="3428">“这一步动作是不是超常发挥了？”</p>
</blockquote>
<p data-start="3430" data-end="3441">然后策略更新就会变成：</p>
<span class="katex-display" style=""><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML" display="block"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi mathvariant="normal">∇</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><mi>log</mi><mo>⁡</mo><msub><mi>π</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>a</mi><mi mathvariant="normal">∣</mi><mi>s</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo><mtext> </mtext><mi>A</mi><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>s</mi><mo separator="true">,</mo><mi>a</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\nabla_\theta \log \pi_\theta(a|s)\, A(s,a)</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 1em; vertical-align: -0.25em;"></span><span class="mord"><span class="mord">∇</span><span class="msupsub"><span class="vlist-t vlist-t2"><span class="vlist-r"><span class="vlist" style="height: 0.3361em;"><span style="top: -2.55em; margin-left: 0em; margin-right: 0.05em;"><span class="pstrut" style="height: 2.7em;"></span><span class="sizing reset-size6 size3 mtight"><span class="mord mathnormal mtight" style="margin-right: 0.02778em;">θ</span></span></span></span><span class="vlist-s">​</span></span><span class="vlist-r"><span class="vlist" style="height: 0.15em;"><span></span></span></span></span></span></span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.1667em;"></span><span class="mop">lo<span style="margin-right: 0.01389em;">g</span></span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.1667em;"></span><span class="mord"><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.03588em;">π</span><span class="msupsub"><span class="vlist-t vlist-t2"><span class="vlist-r"><span class="vlist" style="height: 0.3361em;"><span style="top: -2.55em; margin-left: -0.0359em; margin-right: 0.05em;"><span class="pstrut" style="height: 2.7em;"></span><span class="sizing reset-size6 size3 mtight"><span class="mord mathnormal mtight" style="margin-right: 0.02778em;">θ</span></span></span></span><span class="vlist-s">​</span></span><span class="vlist-r"><span class="vlist" style="height: 0.15em;"><span></span></span></span></span></span></span><span class="mopen">(</span><span class="mord mathnormal">a</span><span class="mord">∣</span><span class="mord mathnormal">s</span><span class="mclose">)</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.1667em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal">A</span><span class="mopen">(</span><span class="mord mathnormal">s</span><span class="mpunct">,</span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.1667em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal">a</span><span class="mclose">)</span></span></span></span></span>
<p data-start="3494" data-end="3534">这比直接拿总奖励更新更靠谱。<br data-start="3508" data-end="3511" />
PPO、A2C、A3C这些，核心味道都在这里。</p>
<hr data-start="3536" data-end="3539" />
<h2 data-section-id="18ium8m" data-start="3541" data-end="3555">你真正该记住的核心逻辑</h2>
<p data-start="3557" data-end="3587">别被公式吓住，policy gradient 就记这个闭环：</p>
<h3 data-section-id="1lcbr37" data-start="3589" data-end="3606">1. 用当前策略去采样动作</h3>
<p data-start="3607" data-end="3620">“我先按现在的习惯做事。”</p>
<h3 data-section-id="wzj1mj" data-start="3622" data-end="3637">2. 看最终结果好不好</h3>
<p data-start="3638" data-end="3648">“这次做得值不值。”</p>
<h3 data-section-id="1tp8isx" data-start="3650" data-end="3670">3. 好结果就增加这些动作的概率</h3>
<p data-start="3671" data-end="3684">“这招有效，以后多来点。”</p>
<h3 data-section-id="vxov20" data-start="3686" data-end="3706">4. 差结果就降低这些动作的概率</h3>
<p data-start="3707" data-end="3718">“这招坑，以后少来。”</p>
<p data-start="3720" data-end="3726">就这么简单。</p>
<hr data-start="3728" data-end="3731" />
<h2 data-section-id="yprgfq" data-start="3733" data-end="3749">一个特别粗暴但很好记的类比</h2>
<p data-start="3751" data-end="3758">你在餐厅点菜。</p>
<p data-start="3760" data-end="3781">你现在对菜单上每道菜都有一个“偏爱概率”。</p>
<p data-start="3783" data-end="3827">第一次你随机点了“红烧牛肉面”，吃完爽飞了。<br data-start="3805" data-end="3808" />
那你以后再来，这道菜的点单概率就上升。</p>
<p data-start="3829" data-end="3863">第二次你点了“奇怪凉拌菜”，难吃哭了。<br data-start="3848" data-end="3851" />
那以后这道菜概率就下降。</p>
<p data-start="3865" data-end="3882">久而久之，你就学会了一个概率分布：</p>
<ul data-start="3884" data-end="3908">
<li data-section-id="1bzk3ns" data-start="3884" data-end="3891">
<p data-start="3886" data-end="3891">最爱点什么</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="1s3u88r" data-start="3892" data-end="3899">
<p data-start="3894" data-end="3899">偶尔点什么</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="ugmuqi" data-start="3900" data-end="3908">
<p data-start="3902" data-end="3908">几乎不点什么</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="3910" data-end="3930">这就是 policy learning。</p>
<p data-start="3932" data-end="3978">而 policy gradient 就是在用“这顿饭吃得爽不爽”的反馈，去调整这个概率分布。</p>
<hr data-start="3980" data-end="3983" />
<h2 data-section-id="8b7gyf" data-start="3985" data-end="4002">你卡住的地方，大概率就在这儿</h2>
<p data-start="4004" data-end="4045">很多人不懂 policy gradient，通常不是因为笨，而是因为脑子里总在问：</p>
<p data-start="4047" data-end="4067"><strong data-start="4047" data-end="4067">“它到底是怎么知道哪个动作好？”</strong></p>
<p data-start="4069" data-end="4073">答案是：</p>
<p data-start="4075" data-end="4105">它<strong data-start="4076" data-end="4085">不是先知道</strong>，它是<strong data-start="4088" data-end="4104">采样出来之后，用结果反推</strong>。</p>
<p data-start="4107" data-end="4115">也就是说它不是：</p>
<blockquote data-start="4117" data-end="4134">
<p data-start="4119" data-end="4134">“我先有标准答案，再照着学。”</p>
</blockquote>
<p data-start="4136" data-end="4139">而是：</p>
<blockquote data-start="4141" data-end="4164">
<p data-start="4143" data-end="4164">“我先试，试完以后根据奖惩慢慢修正自己。”</p>
</blockquote>
<p data-start="4166" data-end="4186">这就是强化学习和监督学习很不一样的地方。</p>
<hr data-start="4188" data-end="4191" />
<h2 data-section-id="whg0k6" data-start="4193" data-end="4210">最后我给你压缩成一句超级白话</h2>
<p data-start="4212" data-end="4231">Policy Gradient 就是：</p>
<p data-start="4233" data-end="4267"><strong data-start="4233" data-end="4267">“把导致高回报的动作概率调大，把导致低回报的动作概率调小。”</strong></p>
<p data-start="4269" data-end="4282">公式只是这句话的数学写法。</p>
<hr data-start="4284" data-end="4287" />
<h2 data-section-id="16etup8" data-start="4289" data-end="4303">你现在先别急着推导公式</h2>
<p data-start="4305" data-end="4324">你这阶段最重要的是先把这三个东西分清：</p>
<ol data-start="4326" data-end="4432">
<li data-section-id="81fsi5" data-start="4326" data-end="4354">
<p data-start="4329" data-end="4354"><strong data-start="4329" data-end="4339">policy</strong>：在状态下选动作的概率分布</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="1jcfukc" data-start="4355" data-end="4387">
<p data-start="4358" data-end="4387"><strong data-start="4358" data-end="4375">return/reward</strong>：这次行为最后好不好</p>
</li>
<li data-section-id="rzm5a5" data-start="4388" data-end="4432">
<p data-start="4391" data-end="4432"><strong data-start="4391" data-end="4410">gradient update</strong>：根据结果，把好动作概率调大，坏动作概率调小</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p data-start="4434" data-end="4492">只要这三个通了，后面 REINFORCE、baseline、advantage、actor-critic 就顺很多。</p>

<span class="katex-display" style=""><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML" display="block"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi mathvariant="normal">∇</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><mi>log</mi><mo>⁡</mo><msub><mi>π</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>a</mi><mi mathvariant="normal">∣</mi><mi>s</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\nabla_\theta \log \pi_\theta(a|s)</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 1em; <blockquote data-start="713" data-end="756">

</blockquote>
<hr data-start="758" data-end="761" />
<h2 data-section-id="1819d13" data-start="763" data-end="782">所以你图里那句话，真正想表达的是</h2>
<p data-start="784" data-end="804">不是在说什么高深东西。<br data-start="795" data-end="798" />

<blockquote data-start="806" data-end="841">
<p data-start="808" data-end="841"><strong data-start="808" data-end="841">“我关心的是：怎样调整参数，能改变这个动作被选中的倾向。”</strong></p>

<ul data-start="851" data-end="892">

<hr data-start="911" data-end="914" />

<p data-start="937" data-end="952">梯度就像一张说明书，上面写着：</p>
<ul data-start="954" data-end="1017">

</li>

</li>
<li data-section-id="wgh1f0" data-start="998" data-end="1017">
<p data-start="1000" data-end="1017">第 4 个旋钮稍微拧大一点效果最好</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p data-start="1019" data-end="1044">所以它不是一个神秘符号。<br data-start="1031" data-end="1034" />
它本质上是在告诉你：</p>
<blockquote data-start="1046" data-end="1064">

</blockquote>
<p data-start="1066" data-end="1074">这里的目标就是：</p>
<span class="katex-display" style=""><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML" display="block"><semantics><mrow><mi>log</mi><mo>⁡</mo><msub><mi>π</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>a</mi><mi mathvariant="normal">∣</mi><mi>s</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\log \pi_\theta(a|s)</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 1em; vertical-align: -0.25em;"></span><span class="mop">lo<span style="margin-right: 0.01389em;">g</span></span><span class="mspace" style="margin-right: 0.1667em;"></span><span class="mord"><span class="mord mathnormal" style="margin-right: 0.03588em;">π</span><span class="msupsub"><span class="vlist-t vlist-t2"><span class="vlist-r"><span class="vlist" style="height: 0.3361em;"><span style="top: -2.55em; margin-left: -0.0359em; margin-right: 0.05em;"><span class="pstrut" style="height: 2.7em;"></span><span class="sizing reset-size6 size3 mtight"><span class="mord mathnormal mtight" style="margin-right: 0.02778em;">θ</span></span></span></span><span class="vlist-s">​</span></span><span class="vlist-r"><span class="vlist" style="height: 0.15em;"><span></span></span></span></span></span></span><span class="mopen">(</span><span class="mord mathnormal">a</span><span class="mord">∣</span><span class="mord mathnormal">s</span><span class="mclose">)</span></span></span></span></span>
<p data-start="1104" data-end="1137">也就是让“动作 <span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>a</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">a</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.4306em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal">a</span></span></span></span> 在状态 <span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML"><semantics><mrow><mi>s</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">s</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 0.4306em;"></span><span class="mord mathnormal">s</span></span></span></span> 下更容易被选中”。</p>
<hr data-start="1139" data-end="1142" />
<h2 data-section-id="ch82q7" data-start="1144" data-end="1155">再说得更直白一点</h2>
<span class="katex-display" style=""><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML" display="block"><semantics><mrow><msub><mi mathvariant="normal">∇</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><mi>log</mi><mo>⁡</mo><msub><mi>π</mi><mi>θ</mi></msub><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mi>a</mi><mi mathvariant="normal">∣</mi><mi>s</mi><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\nabla_\theta \log \pi_\theta(a|s)</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="strut" style="height: 1em;
